Why Visit Europa?

Europa is one Jupiter's many moons (at least 69). It has a water-ice crust and contains an atmosphere composed primarily of oxygen. It's basically Earth!

Here are some facts about Europa:

  • About the size of Earth's moon.
  • Discovered by Galileo Galilei in 1610.
  • Named after Europa, daughter of a greek noblewoman in Greek mythology.
